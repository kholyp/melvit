Best practices in shipping
  • Ship at competitive rates
    Keep your shipping rates low or even ship for free to increase your listing’s visibility and sales.
  • Track every package
    Earn buyers’ trust by tracking your packages and uploading the tracking numbers or giving it to buyer to make shipping seamless.
  • Give buyers a choice
    Attract more shoppers by allowing buyers to choose the shipping service that works best for them. Always communicate perfectly with your buyers and hear from them the best shipping option they would like.
  • Pack your item with care
    Deliver your item in the condition it was described to grow your reputation as a good seller.
  • Get discounts and share the benefits
    Take advantage of exclusive postage discounts with USPS, FedEx and UPS and offer lower shipping rates.
