Melvit Paysafe

M - Paysafe

  • 100% Product Quality Protection
  • 100% on time Shipment Protection
  • 100% Payment Protection
Why M-paysafe?
Pay now

Our aim at melvit is to drive more safe and transparent transaction through our marketplace by enabling buyers and sellers to pay securely through escrow payments. Whether you are offering large scale goods that are manufactured anywhere in africa or handmade items from the lowest of prices we have developed our escrow system to enable you sell securely in a transparent and easy way. Buyers from around the world can trade with any sellers and be 100% guaranteed that their completed orders would be delivered to them as agreed with the seller.

Melvit offers a simple payment experience though our M-paysafe service to power every transaction on ourwebsite.We ensure all sellers get paid and buyers get the goods that they paid for as agreed with the seller during the ordering process. we have partnered with world class institution that is licensed, bonded, and secure, so you can leave the payment challenges to us and focus on building great experiences among your stakeholders on the platform.

As a seller you can opt to receive your payment via any means that would be convenient, We can pay directly to your bank account in your local currency,or via ATM card. All payment is amount dependent as this would determine the best payment currency as the situation arises.

Process Flow
Buyer and Seller agree to transaction terms
Buyer pays to secure account, We accept payment by credit, debit cards and bank transfer as at date.
Seller ships goods and forward the tracking number.
Buyer inspects goods or deliverables and releases funds to Seller
Payment Security and Refund Options
Payment Security
Your money is not released during the trade process until you have confirmed successful delivery of the order, we would also continue to track the item to ensure that its being delivered to you. Once confirmed, M-paysafe will release payment to the supplier. All of your information will be safely encrypted without any third party interference.
Full Refund
If the supplier doesn't ship your order on time, or if you don't receive it and it is determined to be the fault of the supplier, you'll get your payment returned directly.
Partial Refund & Keeping Products
If the products you receive are significantly different from the product requirements agreed on within the contract, you may choose to receive a partial refund and keep the products. Please note that situation varies, we implore you to have a valid contract with the seller, contract can include signed PO, invoice e.t.c
Resolving Disputes
  • 1
    Contact the supplier
  • 2
    Open a dispute to get a refund
  • 3
    Escalate the dispute to m-Paysafe

If your order hasn't arrived within the agreed timeframe, or is delivered but isn't as described, contact the supplier via email. Most suppliers will quickly resolve any issues. Also, track the item online or contact the shipping company delivering the item to know that the item is on its way. We implore you to develop good relationship with your seller to facilitate good communication lines.

If you were unable to resolve the issue with the supplier, you can submit a refund request by clicking Open Dispute before the applicable deadline. This function is available 5 days after the shipping date, and allows you to formally discuss solutions with the supplier.

If you are not satisfied with the supplier’s solution, you can Escalate the dispute to our Customer Service Team. We will mediate between you and the supplier to resolve the problem.

We accept payment by credit card, debit cards and bank transfer. We would continue to add more payment options as we proceed into the future.
As a flexible, growth oriented and forward thinking organization we welcome batter solutions to make our payment platform easy. If you are an organization that can provide us with other payment platform, do not hesitate to speak to us.
