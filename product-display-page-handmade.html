The 85mm F1.8 lenses are manual focus primes, designed to give exceptional results at wide apertures. At F1.8 great images can be obtained, which is perfect for portraiture and low light shooting. The Kelda 85 mm F1.8 lens should be a serious consideration to all professional or amateur photographers in need of a fast yet intricate prime lens at a reasonable price.
